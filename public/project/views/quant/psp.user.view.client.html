Exam Instructions

The exam consists of 10 multiple-choice questions, each presented on a separate page. The complete instructions for the exam are available from every page, so you can always check them at any time. You can use the sample question as a reference for the instructions. Note, however, that the buttons on the sample page are not active.

The instructions can be summarized as follows:

  • You select an answer by clicking on the checkbox to the left of your choice. You can change your answer as often as you like simply by selecting a different checkbox.

  • When you have made a choice for your answer press the submit button to submit, upur answer and press the next button to jump to the "Next Problem".

  • When you have finished, press the "Turn in Exam" button to end taking the exam. Note that your answer is recorded whenever you move to a new problem (or turn in the exam).

You can use the email address at the bottom of every page to contact the Math Department if you have technical difficulties with the exam.
