Quant


The Quantitative aptitude section of the GRE contains 20 questions (35 mins) which of three types:
Multiple choice problem solving
Numeric entry
 Quantitative comparisons
 You may be tested on basic arithmetic, algebra, geometry and a few miscellaneous topics (mainly data interpretation and applied math).

Verbal


The verbal reasoning section of the GRE contains 20 questions (30 mins) per section which of three types:
Sentence Equivalence
Text Completion
Reading Comprehension
 A good vocabulary is essential for the Text Completion and Sentence Equivalence questions which account for approximately 50% of the questions in this section.

AWA


GRE sets two writing tasks collectively called the Analytical Writing Section which of two types:
Analyze an Issue
Analyze an Argument
 The tasks are designed to test critical thinking and analytical writing skills. The essays come first on the test - 30 minutes for the issue and 30 minutes for the argument.
